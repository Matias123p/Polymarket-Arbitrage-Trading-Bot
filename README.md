## 📊 How the Bot Works

The bot checks markets for a spread between prices. When it finds a gap, it tracks the opportunity and helps you act on it based on your settings.

The spread maker strategy can help you:

- Watch both sides of a market
- Spot price gaps
- Set order levels
- Reduce time spent checking markets by hand
- Keep your rules in one place

It is built for users who want a clear view of market moves without watching every chart on their own.

## ⚙️ Main Features

### 🔎 Arbitrage Opportunity Tracking

The bot scans for price gaps that may create an arbitrage setup. It watches for differences that can matter when you want to enter and exit a market with care.

### 📐 Spread Maker Strategy

The app supports a spread maker flow, which helps you place orders around a price range instead of chasing one price point. This gives you more control over entry and exit levels.

### 🧾 Simple Market View

The interface is built for plain use. You can check key values, see current spreads, and review the markets that matter most to you.

### 🧮 Risk Controls

You can set trade limits, so the bot stays within your range. This helps you keep order size and market exposure under control.

### 📝 Log Output

The app can show activity logs so you can see what it checked, what it found, and what action it took.

## 📌 Example Settings

These are common settings you may see in a spread maker bot:

- Market list: the markets you want to track
- Entry spread: the gap that starts a trade

- Order size: the amount per trade
